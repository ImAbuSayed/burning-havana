Shishe vich takkda haan ajj, seeing my scars
Main karda haan gallaan with the shooting stars
Tu jhoothe laare laaye mainu, breaking my trust
Main ban gaya haan raakh ajj, turning to dust
Par ajj main uthda haan, feeling so bold
Mera dil ban gaya ae hun, freezing cold
Mud ke na aavin tu, in the cold rain
Main jit gaya haan ajj, beating the pain
